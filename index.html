Our Story

Since 2010, Le Petit Four has been crafting exquisite French pastries and desserts in the heart of the city. Our passion for authentic French baking traditions, combined with innovative techniques, creates an unforgettable culinary experience.

Each creation is meticulously prepared by our master pastry chefs, trained in the finest culinary schools of Paris. We use only premium ingredients, from locally sourced butter to imported French chocolate.
